📋 Factors Beyond the Numbers

Reasons to BUY

  • ✅ Building equity and net worth
  • ✅ Stability — fixed payment, can't be evicted
  • ✅ Freedom to renovate and customize
  • ✅ Forced savings / wealth-building discipline
  • ✅ Hedge against rent inflation

Reasons to RENT

  • ✅ Flexibility to move at any time
  • ✅ No maintenance responsibility
  • ✅ No property value risk
  • ✅ Lower upfront cash required
  • ✅ Better if staying fewer than 3–5 years
