📘 Metric Definitions

Cap Rate — NOI ÷ Purchase Price. Measures property yield before financing. 5–7% is typical for stabilized multifamily.
Cash-on-Cash Return — Annual levered cash flow ÷ equity invested. Shows your cash yield on actual dollars deployed.
IRR — Internal rate of return over the full hold period including sale. The most complete return metric. Target 12–18% for value-add.
