Extra Payment Strategy

💡 Strategy tip

Even $100–$200 extra per month can save you tens of thousands in interest over the life of a 30-year loan.

📐 How Extra Payments Work

Goes to Principal

Extra payments are applied directly to your loan principal, reducing the balance faster and cutting future interest charges.

Compounding Effect

Every dollar of principal paid early saves you 20–30 years of interest on that dollar. Small amounts add up dramatically.

No Penalty

Most conventional mortgages have no prepayment penalty. Always confirm with your lender, but for most homeowners it's free to pay extra.
