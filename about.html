About REProforma

REProforma is a real estate financial analysis platform built to help people make smarter real estate decisions. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, renter deciding whether to buy, or a real estate investor analyzing a deal, REProforma provides tools that use realistic financial assumptions instead of overly simple online calculators.

Homebuyer Tools

Affordability calculators, rent vs buy analysis, and mortgage planning tools designed for everyday buyers.

Investment Tools

Multifamily, commercial, and land development financial models used by real estate professionals.

Market Research

Local housing affordability dashboards and real estate market research tools.

How Our Calculations Work

Many online calculators only consider the mortgage payment. REProforma tools attempt to include real-world housing costs such as property taxes, insurance, maintenance, HOA fees, and opportunity cost of down payment. These tools are designed to give users a more realistic picture of housing affordability and long-term financial impact.

REProforma is an educational and financial analysis platform. It is not a lender, broker, or financial advisor. All results are estimates based on user inputs and assumptions.

Founder

REProforma was created by Dhruv, a student interested in real estate, finance, technology, and community development. The platform started as a project to understand real estate investment analysis and grew into a broader platform focused on making financial tools more accessible to everyday users.

Dhruv is also the founder of The Deep Roots Project, a sustainability initiative focused on environmental awareness and tree planting in local communities.
