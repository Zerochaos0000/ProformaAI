Payoff Strategies

Round Up Your Payment

If your payment is $1,847, round to $2,000. The extra $153/mo adds up to years off your mortgage.

Biweekly Payments

Pay half your monthly payment every 2 weeks. You'll make 26 half-payments = 13 full payments/year — one extra per year.

Apply Windfalls

Tax refunds, bonuses, and raises applied to principal early in the loan have the biggest impact on total interest.

Refinance Consideration

If rates drop 1%+, refinancing to a shorter term can dramatically cut total interest, especially in early years.
